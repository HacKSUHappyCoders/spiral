Spiral

Controls:
• Left mouse: Orbit
• Shift + Left mouse: Pan
• Scroll: Zoom
• Click a building to inspect its data
• Double-click to warp into its sub-spiral galaxy
• A / D: Step to previous / next node
• W: Warp into selected node's sub-galaxy
• S: Warp back out to parent level
• Click again or press Close Inspector to dismiss
• Panoramic Render to see all galaxies at once
